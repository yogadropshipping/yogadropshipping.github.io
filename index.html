What’s Drop Shipping?

  • Drop shipping is when you sell products that you don't have to inventory or ship, because you’ve made arrangements with a company who does it all for you. If you have clients/friends that would be interested in yoga related products, WE ARE THAT COMPANY, and we would like to make you our partner.
  • Take advantage of our DISCOUNTED PARTNER PRICES and set the price for your own customers. We handle the shipment and any returns, so you never have to handle the products. Our warehouse is "your" warehouse.


How does our drop ship program work?

  • Once your account is approved and set up, simply add product pictures and details to your website and start taking orders. When you receive an order, simply email us the info. We process the order and drop ship our products from our warehouse to your customers. Once the order process has been completed, we will send you an email with the tracking number so you can provide it to your customer.
